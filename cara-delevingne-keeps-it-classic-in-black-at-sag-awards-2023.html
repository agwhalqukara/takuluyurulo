    17 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Cara Delevingne delivers classic glamour at the 2023 SAG Awards on Sunday (February 26) in Los Angeles.

    The 30-year-old Carnival Row actress and model arrived at Fairmont Century Plaza for the awards ceremony wearing a black gown with long sleeves and a plunging neckline that was enhanced with a floral detail.

    She threw the dress for the cameras, creating a windblown effect on the carpet.

    Read more about Cara Delevingne’s 2023 SAG Awards look…

    Cara kept her hair simple, slicking it back and styling it in waves. She accessorized with a diamond necklace and matching earrings. A bold red lip provided a pop of color, and she kept her makeup simple and smoky.

    Going into the ceremony, Cara is up for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series alongside her Only Murders in the Building castmates. Keep an eye on the site, and we’ll let you know who takes the win home later tonight!
