    What a year! Homeland's Corey Stoll is engaged to his actress girlfriend Nadia Bowers, Us Weekly can exclusively confirm.

    Stoll, 38, has had a busy couple of years. The actor became a household name with his role as Peter Russo in the Netflix political drama House of Cards, and has had major roles in Homeland and The Strain.

    "Corey and Nadia are such a great couple, she's an amazing support to him," an insider tells Us. "Wasn't shocked when I heard they had gotten engaged."

    The source adds: "Everyone is super happy for them."
