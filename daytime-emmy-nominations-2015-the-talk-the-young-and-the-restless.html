    Daytime Emmy Nominations 2015: The Talk, The Young and the Restless

    They really like you! The nominees for the 2015 Daytime Emmy Awards were announced live on The Talk on Tuesday, March 31, and plenty of your favorite soap operas, talk shows, and game shows made the list.

    28 Aug 2024, 00:00

    They really like you! The nominees for the 2015 Daytime Emmy Awards were announced live on The Talk on Tuesday, March 31, and plenty of your favorite soap operas, talk shows, and game shows made the list.

    On the soap front, Days of Our Lives, The Young and the Restless, and General Hospital nabbed the most nominations, with Alison Sweeney scoring her first-ever nod. That’s right, after more than two decades on Days Sweeney has finally been recognized for playing Sami Brady on the long-running soap.

    Find out which other actors — and talk show hosts and morning shows and animated shows — made the cut below, and tune in to the awards on Subnday, April 26 at 8 p.m. ET on Pop Network. The full nominee list can be found on the Daytime Emmys website.
