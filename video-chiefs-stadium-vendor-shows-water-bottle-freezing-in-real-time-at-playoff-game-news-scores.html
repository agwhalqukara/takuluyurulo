    Good luck trying to get a bottle of water at Arrowhead Stadium on Saturday night during the AFC Wild Card round matchup between the Kansas City Chiefs and Miami Dolphins.

    Prior to kickoff, a vendor at the stadium shared a video of a bottle of water freezing just seconds after being pulled out of a refrigerator:

    Saturday's wild card matchup in Kansas City will be played in subzero temperatures, and even windows in suites are freezing to the point where fans can't see out of them.

    While some, including former NFL quarterback Robert Griffin III, called for the game to be moved due to the dangerously cold temperatures, the only wild card matchup that was postponed this weekend was the Buffalo Bills versus Pittsburgh Steelers.

    The NFL postponed the game between the Bills and Steelers at Highmark Stadium due to a major snowstorm that could drop up to three feet of snow in certain parts of western New York.

    The two teams will now fight for a berth in the divisional round on Monday afternoon with kickoff set for 4:30 p.m. ET.
