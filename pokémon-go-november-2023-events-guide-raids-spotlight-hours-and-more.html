    Niantic has revealed an exciting schedule for the upcoming month of November 2023, just as October 2023 closes up its Halloween celebrations in Pokémon Go. We’ve got a first look at what’s in store for Pokémon trainers this exciting new month thanks to an eye-catching infographic that Niantic shared on their official Twitter account. November 2023 looks to be an exciting month filled with exciting Pokémon encounters, action-packed raids, and a variety of special events. What awaits us in the upcoming month? Let’s discover everything as we delve deeper into the fascinating world of Pokémon Go. Pokémon Go November 2023 Events Guide: Raids, Spotlight Hours, and More.

    Navigating the Pokémon Go November 2023 Landscape

    Trainers can now get a tantalising peek of what’s to come in November 2023 thanks to Niantic’s latest masterful image shared on Twitter. Excitement is in the air, especially with major events like Community Day and Fashion Week coming up. We have put together an extensive event guide for November 2023 to make sure you don’t miss anything and to help you keep track of everything.

    https://x.com/PokemonGoApp/status/1716562956421357778?s=20

    November 2023 Pokémon Go Events: A Closer Look

    As November approaches, Pokémon Go enthusiasts can eagerly anticipate a series of captivating events. Here’s a quick overview of what’s scheduled:

    • Dia de Muertos (Day of the Dead): A celebration that pays tribute to departed Pokémon.
    • Community Day: A special day dedicated to community bonding and catching unique Pokémon.
    • Festival of Lights: A radiant event that lights up the world of Pokémon Go.
    • Raid Day: Prepare for epic battles and fantastic rewards.
    • Fashion Week: A chance to flaunt your stylish Pokémon.
    • Party Up!: Get ready to party with your favorite Pokémon.
    • Community Day Classic: A nostalgic twist on a fan-favorite event.

    For specific event dates and times, consult the table below:

    Event, Date and Time

    • Dia de Muertos (Day of the Dead): November 1 – November 2 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Community Day: November 5 (2:00 PM – 5:00 PM)
    • Festival of Lights: November 7 – November 12 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Raid Day: November 11 (2:00 PM – 5:00 PM)
    • Fashion Week: November 15 – November 19 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Party Up!: November 22 – November 27 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Community Day Classic: November 25 (2:00 PM – 5:00 PM)

    Pokémon Go Revealing Raid Hours in November

    Wednesdays are Raid Hour, a much-anticipated worldwide event in the Pokémon Go world. Certain Pokémon—which may be uncommon, legendary, or mythical—appear more frequently as Raid Bosses during Raid Hours. Raid bouts become far more accessible and rare moves are frequently introduced during this event. The following Pokémon are scheduled to appear during the November Raid Hours from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM local time, so mark your calendars:

    Pokémon and Date

    • Darkrai: November 1
    • Genesect: November 8
    • Virizion: November 15
    • Coballio: November 22
    • Terrakion: November 29

    Pokémon Go Spotlight Hours

    A special chance to concentrate on a different Pokémon every day is provided by Spotlight Hours for trainers. The benefits and perks you receive for capturing these featured Pokémon are exceptional. In November, between 6:00 and 7:00 p.m. local time, the following Pokémon are expected to be featured:

    Pokémon, Bonus and Date

    • Chincho: 2× Candy for transferring Pokémon – November 7
    • Buneary: 2× XP for evolving Pokémon – November 14
    • Dunsparce: 2× Stardust for catching Pokémon – November 21
    • Lechonk: 2× XP for catching Pokémon – November 28

    Research Breakthrough

    Trainers can earn substantial rewards by completing a variety of chores in Pokémon Go’s long-standing Field Research feature. The start time of this event is 1:00 PM PDT (1:30 AM IST) on September 1, 2023, and it ends at 1:00 PM PDT (1:30 AM IST) on December 1. Finish Field Research assignments and keep an eye out for encounters with the following Pokémon to unlock Research Breakthrough encounters:

    • Galarian Farfetch’d
    • Larvitar
    • Sableye
    • Bagon
    • Furfrou
    • Goomy

    Pokéstop Showcases in November

    Trainers have the ideal chance to display their Pokémon’s abilities at Pokéstop Showcases. Trainers can compete against one another at these showcases, which are hosted at specific PokéStops. The times of the upcoming Pokéstop Showcases have been changed to reflect local time zones and will take place on the dates indicated.

    Pokéstop Showcases, Date and Time

    • Duskull: November 1 – November 2 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Gothita Mareanie: November 15 – November 19 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Litwick: November 7 – November 10 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • ???: November 11 – November 12 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Sprigatito Fuecoco Quaxly: November 22 – November 27 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Mareep Ampharos: November 25 (2:00 PM – 5:00 PM)
    • Sprigatito Fuecoco Quaxly: November 26 – November 27 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)
    • Lechonk: November 28 (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM)

    Note that Niantic has not yet made an official announcement, despite the fact that it is widely assumed that Mega Gallade would be the mysterious Pokémon during the Mega Raid from November 12 to November 16. With so much to look forward to in November 2023, it’s sure to be an exciting month for Pokémon Go players. Check back soon for additional information, and don’t forget to mark your calendar for all the exciting events that trainers throughout the globe will be involved in this November.
