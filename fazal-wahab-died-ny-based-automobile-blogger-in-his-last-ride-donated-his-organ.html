    Fazal Wahab (46) passed away at NY, USA. The whole hospital came to thank him as he donated his organs. Saved three lives. He was elder brother of Sahab Qamar, my son in law. Please pray for him.

    Fazal Wahab was more than just an automobile blogger; he was a passionate advocate for the industry, providing insightful commentary and analysis on the latest trends and developments. His insightful articles and reviews helped thousands of readers make informed decisions about their automobile purchases.

    Over the years, Fazal Wahab made significant contributions to the automotive world through his blog. He covered a wide range of topics, from car safety to new model releases.
