    About

    Valley Vocational College (VVC) is a community college located in San Bernardino County, California, USA. It offers a variety of certificate and degree programs in fields such as nursing, automotive technology, welding, and business administration. VVC aims to provide affordable and accessible education to the local community, and has been serving students since 1946. The college has a diverse student population and a range of support services to help students succeed academically and professionally.
