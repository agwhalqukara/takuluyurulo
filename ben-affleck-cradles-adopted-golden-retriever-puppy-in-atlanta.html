    Rockabye puppy! Ben Affleck brought a furry member of the family to Atlanta with him on Monday, July 20.

    The Batman v Superman actor, 42, was snapped cradling a fluffy puppy in his arms like an infant on Monday. He flew down to Atlanta to spend time with his three kids, Violet, 9, Seraphina, 6, and Samuel, 3, who are currently staying with their mom, Jennifer Garner, as she films her latest flick, Miracles From Heaven.

    Affleck, still wearing his wedding band, was dressed down on Monday in a blue T-shirt, zip-up hoodie, and a baseball cap, while sporting his trademark scruff.

    As previously reported by Us, Garner and the kids are currently living together in a five-bedroom rental property in the Georgia city.

    The puppy, meanwhile, has also been enduring the split after it was adopted this past May. As reported by Us in the June 29 issue, Affleck and Garner took their daughters to a dog adoption in the Pacific Palisades this spring, shortly before the high-profile couple announced their split in an official joint statement on June 30.

    According to an eyewitness at the shelter, Affleck scowled at Garner when she encouraged their daughters to play with a litter of golden retriever puppies. The actor also was rude to a female volunteer.
