    Jessie Murph is a rising star in the music industry and social media. She has gained millions of fans and followers for her catchy songs and lip sync videos on TikTok. But how old is she and what is her background? Here are some facts about Jessie Murph’s age and biography.

    Early Life and Career

    Jessie Murph was born on September 22, 2004 in the United States, according to Famous Birthdays. She is currently 19 years old as of 2023. She grew up in Huntsville, Alabama, where she was a competitive cheerleader, according to al2. She started making TikTok videos in July of 2019, where she and a friend Googled how to become famous in an early video, according to Famous Birthdays.

    She soon gained popularity for her singing and vlogging content, as well as her covers of popular songs by artists like Hozier, Billie Eilish, and Olivia Rodrigo. She also collaborated with other TikTok stars like Chase Hudson, Noah Beck, and Dixie D’Amelio. She has over 9 million followers on her jessiemurphhh account, according to Famous Birthdays.

    Music and Success

    Jessie Murph signed a record deal with Columbia Records and Disruptor Records in 2021, according to Women In Pop. She released her debut single “Upgrade” in the same year, which was a viral hit on TikTok and Spotify. She followed it up with four more singles in 2021: “Always Been You”, “Texas” (featuring Maren Morris), “Pray”, and “How Could You”. Her song “Always Been You” has over 70 million streams on Spotify, according to Famous Birthdays, and was certified gold by the RIAA and platinum by Music Canada, according to Wikipedia.

    In 2022, she released her debut EP “Drowning”, which received positive reviews from critics and fans. Her song “Pray” charted in several countries, including the UK, Ireland, Norway, and New Zealand, according to Wikipedia. She also performed at the Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival in 2022, according to al.

    In 2023, she collaborated with Diplo and Polo G on the song “Heartbroken”, which was part of Diplo’s album “Diplo Presents Thomas Wesley, Chapter 2: Swamp Savant”. The song charted on the US Billboard Hot 100 and the Canadian Hot 100, according to Wikipedia.

    Personal Life and Future Plans

    Jessie Murph has not revealed much about her personal life or relationship status. She has featured her mom in some of her videos, according to Famous Birthdays. She is also close friends with fellow singer and TikTok star Nessa Barrett, according to Nextau.

    Jessie Murph has expressed her desire to continue making music and exploring different genres and styles. She has also said that she wants to inspire and empower her fans with her songs and messages, according to Women In Pop. She has been praised for her authenticity, talent, and charisma by fans and critics alike. She is one of the most promising and influential young artists of her generation.
