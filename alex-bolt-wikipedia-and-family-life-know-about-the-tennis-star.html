    Alex Bolt Wiki: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Height

    Alex Bolt was born in 1993 in Murray Bridge, South Australia. He is 31 years of age and he blows out his birthday candles on the 5th of January every year.

    Alex started playing tennis at the age of seven. Apart from that, he was also involved in Australian rules football and basketball in his youth. Alex even played local football for the Mypolonga Tigers in the River Murray Football League.

    With Australian nationality, Alex has white ethnicity. He is of Russian descent from his father’s side. The tennis has a tall height of 6 feet (1.83 meters).

    Alex Bolt Tennis Career Earnings and Net Worth

    Alex is currently ranked 310th in singles in the Tennis World Rankings. His career-high singles ranking came at World No. 125 on 4 March 2019.

    Apart from that, Alex couldn’t move past the qualifiers in his seven attempts at the French Open. Likewise, he also could only play the qualifier 3 of the US Open in 2018.
