    Anya Rangaswami was born between 1991 and 1994 in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. She and her husband live in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. Anya’s full name is Anya Rangaswami Sait.

    Career

    Anya has always been interested in design since she was in elementary school. In 2010, she worked as a Design intern at Dev Kabir Malik after finishing her studies. She worked as a project intern at Ray+Keshavan and The Brand Union the following year. She’s also worked as an associate designer at the same company.

    She decided to start her own business in 2013. Between 2013 and 2014, she relocates to Mumbai, Maharashtra, to begin working as a freelancer. In Bombay, she worked on brand design, writing, identity design, and research projects. She has worked in various departments and fields, including film, hospitality, music, infrastructure, and real estate.

    Anya has been working in the design field for about 8-9 years and has a lot of experience. All of the various aid her growth. She returned to her hometown of Bengaluru in 2015 and founded her own company, Rheson. Her lovely designs can be found on her official website.
